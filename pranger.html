The Pink Ranger

Bio

Kimberly Ann Hart

Kimberly is a bright and beautiful girl who loves mirrors, shopping malls and gymnastics. At school she is always the most popular girl and loves the attention she receives. Deep down she is strong and independent and has always longed for adventure and danger. She is always positive when she's with her friends and saves her sarcasm for the bad guys. Kimberly is a champion gymnast and brings this skill with her into her life as a Power Ranger. She works hard at it and is able to jump out of almost any situation. Kimberly draws power from the Pterodactyl. Her weapon is the Power Bow.

Weapon

Power Bow

This is the personal weapon of the Pink Ranger. It fires projectile pink arrows at enemies, something Kimberly often does from midair. The arrows themselves are explosive on impact. It can be used as a melee weapon as well. Kimberly also calls her weapon the Battle Bow. It can transform into a harp also.

Zord

Pterodactyl

The Pterodacyl zord forms the chestplate of the Megazord. It does not combine with the Dragonzord Battle Mode. When on its own it can fire twin lasers, which look like lightning. This is the only Dinozord with flight capability. It remains submerged in a volcano.
