The Black Ranger

Bio

Zack Taylor

Zack is a kid in love with life. He is everybody's friend and lights up a room when he enters it. He's also a kid with a good heart and a seemingly boundless supply of energy. He is smooth talking, streetwise, very clever and has the ability to disarm his adversaries with a quick smile and a smooth line. He fancies himself to be a ladies man. Zack's passion in life is music and dance. He is also an accomplished gymnast, a skill which he combines with dancing and martial arts to form a karate style he calls Hip Hop Kido. Zack draws his power from the Mastodon. His weapon is the Power Axe.

Weapon

Power Axe

This is the personal weapon of the Black Ranger. It is a black, single-blade axe to be used for blunt damage. The base of the axe can be slid downward towards the base, converting into a cannon ike weapon that forms the main portion of the Power Blaster. Zack can also use it as a cannon on its own. Zack also calls his weapon the Cosmic Cannon.
